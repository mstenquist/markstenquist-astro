# O2X

A comprehensive human performance optimization platform designed for tactical athletes and first responders.

## Project Overview

O2X delivers critical performance training and mental health resources to those who serve in high-stress, high-stakes environments including military, fire, and law enforcement personnel.

## Key Achievements

- Built performance tracking and analytics systems
- Integrated real-time biometric data processing
- Developed mental health and training resources
- Delivered mission-critical React applications
